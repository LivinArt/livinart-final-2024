Get Know More About Livin QR.

Livin QR offers a range of features to ensure safety and convenience in various situations. Firstly, during emergencies, Livin QR swiftly connects users with 108 Ambulance services and 100 Dial, drastically reducing response times to mere seconds. Additionally, it streamlines the process of recovering lost items through a user-friendly interface, minimizing inconvenience for users. Furthermore, Livin QR addresses parental concerns by introducing QR tags for school bags, aiding in quick identification and reporting during unexpected events. Moreover, it enhances medical care by facilitating the submission of victims' medical histories to hospitals, ensuring timely and informed treatment. Operating on a subscription-based model, Livin QR provides flexible plans with annual and 6-month options at competitive prices, with an in-app payment gateway simplifying the renewal process securely and hassle-free. Finally, Livin QR integrates user-provided insurance information, collaborating with external insurance providers to offer additional value.

In summary, Livin QR offers a comprehensive solution for safety and convenience. It ensures swift emergency responses, streamlines lost item recovery, enhances child safety, and facilitates medical care through the submission of medical histories. With its subscription-based model and in-app payment gateway, Livin QR provides accessible and secure services to users. Additionally, its integration with insurance providers offers supplementary benefits, further enhancing its value proposition.
